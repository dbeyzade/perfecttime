Your privacy is important to us. PerfecTime is designed with your privacy in mind.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how PerfecTime ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our meeting time management application.

2. Information We Collect

PerfecTime collects minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Email address for authentication (via Supabase)
  • Meeting Data: Meeting names, durations, and timestamps you create
  • Subscription Information: Purchase history for premium features (via RevenueCat)

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification beyond email
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts or calendar
  • We do not collect browsing history
  • We do not sell your data to third parties

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Sync your meeting data across devices
  • Process subscription payments
  • Send important service-related notifications
  • Improve and optimize the App

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using:

  • Supabase: For user authentication and meeting data (encrypted, secure cloud storage)
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management
  • Local Device: App preferences stored securely on your device

All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard TLS/SSL protocols.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database services - Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management - Privacy Policy
  • Apple: App Store and payment processing - Privacy Policy

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

9. Children's Privacy

PerfecTime is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
